I want to share with you some of my pieces! Please be kind and don't criticize me mwhehhsheh

"A Beauty that goes Forever"

Your beauty is like the rotation of the earth

It goes forever

You possessed it since birth

It just gets brighter

Your eyes mesmerize me

Your hair is the smoothest

Your voice is soothing

Your smile is the sweetest

Everything about you is perfect

Just like the Orion's belt

You're like an angel that came from heaven

That's why you are so hard to neglect

Having you as an inspiration is amazing

Every time I see you, butterflies attack me

Your beauty is like no other

A beauty that goes forever

"Her"

Staring here

Hugs the cold breeze

Its raining

Let me feel heat

Walking down on her building

Be safe please

As she walks with someone

It hurts jeez

Approximately a kilometer

As usual I see her

An umbrella with her hand

With a boy that's a little bit taller

As they walk on a moist terrain

I go back inside with a bit of pain

Thinking all the memories I gain

How I look stupid and lame

"Unknown Feeling"

Recently, I feel anew

A feeling that's unknown

Yet so familiar

Like talking to the moon

What feeling is this?

A nostalgia? A memory?

Sometimes a feeling of bliss

Sometimes a feeling of tragedy

With all the uncertainty

I sure feel empty

This unknown feeling won't go away

I just want to go astray
